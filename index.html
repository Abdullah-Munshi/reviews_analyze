About Feefo

Feefo helps both consumers and businesses make the right decisions.    Founded in 2010, Feefo works with 6,500+ brands worldwide to collect reliable and constructive reviews they can learn from and display. We invite verified customers to leave feedback, which results in reliable, fake-free reviews, so consumers can learn how people like them feel about different products and services. And companies can discover what they’re doing right, and where they can improve. This allows Feefo’s clients to create transparent, trusted relationships and deliver exceptional services that their customers can depend on - every time.    We are proud to work with companies, large and small, from household names to local heroes.
